Why v2. v1 ranks pairs for low correlation then trades their spread as if it reverts — a contradiction, since only cointegrated legs revert. v2 flips that: it only trades a pair whose spread is statistically mean-reverting, sizes leg B = β × leg A (money-matched), and cuts a position that hasn't reverted within ~2× its half-life.
