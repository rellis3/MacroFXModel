🎯 NQ-QMR BT → 📊 Macro Equity BT → Risk Hub ← Dashboard
Why this exists: WALCL (Fed balance sheet) and WTREGEN (TGA) on FRED only update weekly — too slow to explain a system that trades 2-3x/week off "repo and reverse repo" fundamentals. This page pulls the genuinely-daily pieces instead — ON RRP usage (FRED RRPONTSYD) and the Treasury General Account balance from Treasury's Daily Treasury Statement — and tests whether day-over-day drains/injections in those two line up with next-day NQ moves, with Tue/Thu T-bill settlement days called out separately since that's where Treasury cash-management flows cluster.
This is a diagnostic, not a trading signal. It's checking a hypothesis raised while comparing notes against another NQ system, not validating an edge — read the correlation/hit-rate numbers as "is there anything here worth digging into further", not as a backtest result.
