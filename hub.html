Portfolio DD Gate
If combined book drops >8% from peak, all books reduce size 50% until recovery above −4%. Prevents compounding drawdowns across correlated stress events.
P1 Macro Regime Overlay
P1 composite score gates all equity-correlated books. Score >+0.5: full size. Score −0.5 to +0.5: 75%. Score <−0.5: 50% or flat for P4/P5.
