Monitors USD/JPY 3-day acceleration + VIX + HY spreads. Fires when 2 of 3 breach threshold. Kills carry, halves equity/momentum.
Portfolio DD Gate
If combined book drops >8% from peak, all books reduce size by 50% until recovery above −4%. Prevents compounding drawdowns.
Macro Regime Overlay (P1 Score)
P1 composite score gates NQ system and P4/P5 sizing. Score > +0.5: full size. Score −0.5 to +0.5: 75%. Score < −0.5: 50% or flat.
VIX Regime Gate
VIX Z-score (60-day). LOW (<−0.5): full carry, full NQ. NORMAL (−0.5 to 1.0): 75% carry. HIGH (>1.0): exit carry, reduce NQ 50%. EXTREME (>2.0): all books 25%.
NQ Gate 1 — Asia Data

NQ Gate 2 — London Confirmation
Fires ~12:40 London (07:40 ET). London session must confirm Asia direction. Both gates required for entry at 09:25 ET.
