Forecast Level Analyser

Measures what price does at each forecast line — no trading. Reversion vs continuation, per line, per slice.
daily weekly 20d
Lines: Proj H/L 75p & med (expected high/low), Close 75p & med (expected close displacement) — both sides of the open. Reverted = after touching a line, price returned to the next line inward (toward open) before reaching the next line outward; continued = reached the next line outward first. n = decided touches (with an intraday path). Cells with n < 30 are dimmed — too few to trust. Daily uses M1 from R2; nothing here is a trade — no costs, no stops.
