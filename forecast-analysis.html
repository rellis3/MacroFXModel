Forecast Level Analyser

What price actually does at each vol-forecast line — reversion vs continuation, with statistical significance. No trades, no costs.
daily weekly 20d
Significance: each reversion rate is tested against 50/50 (binomial z).  green  = price reverts off the line significantly more than chance (a fade edge);  red  = continues through significantly more (a breakout edge); grey = indistinguishable from a coin-flip. n = decided touches; cells with n<30 are dimmed. Reverted = returned to the next line inward before reaching the next line outward. Magnitudes are % of price (comparable across pairs). Daily uses M1 from R2; nothing here is a trade.
