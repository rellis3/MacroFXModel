System Logic: Gate 1 reads Fed Net Liquidity (WALCL − WTREGEN − RRPON), %-change, 5-day publication lag, rolling 252-day z-score — z > +threshold → LONG bias, z < −threshold → SHORT bias, otherwise NEUTRAL (no trade). Gate 2 checks whether the yield curve (T10Y2Y) and HY credit spread (BAMLH0A0HYM2, inverted) z-scores agree with Gate 1's direction — "require both" (stricter) or "require either". Entries fire at NY open (the OANDA H1 candle starting 13:00 UTC EDT / 14:00 UTC EST — same bar NQ-QMR uses for its 09:25 ET entry) using yesterday's gate status (no lookahead); exits fire at NY open on a gate flip, or at the day's 5pm-NY close if the close-based stop is hit. The stop check is once-daily by design (matching the daily cadence of the gate signal itself), not a data limitation.
