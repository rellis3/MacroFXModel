Weekly Vol Range Backtester
Monday-anchored · EWMA vol × √5 · Fade HL50/HL75 → Monday open · 26 pairs
Run Weekly Backtest:
How it works — Every Monday the engine computes EWMA volatility from all prior daily closes, scales to weekly (σw = σd × √5), and marks 4 levels from Monday open: HL50 ± = median expected weekly range (BM 50th %ile × corr × σw)  |  HL75 ± = 75th %ile weekly range. Rev-HL50 ★: Entry limit at HL50, TP = Monday open, SL = HL75. R:R ≈ 3.8:1 (same geometry as daily Rev-HL50).  |  Rev-HL75: Entry at HL75, TP = Monday open, SL = HL75 × slMult. Outcomes are resolved bar-by-bar through Tuesday–Friday; open trades force-close at Friday's EOD close.
